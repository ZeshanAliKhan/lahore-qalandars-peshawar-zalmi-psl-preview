Matchup Notes

The three phases that can decide Lahore vs Peshawar.

Powerplay

Lahore need a far cleaner start than in the first meeting. Peshawar need enough stability to keep home momentum quiet.

Middle overs

This is where Lahore have to stay connected and where Zalmi will try to repeat the squeeze that made the first game one-sided.

Death overs

The finish should favor the side that keeps wickets and clarity. If Lahore are still rebuilding late, Zalmi will be comfortable again.

My personal read is that Lahore need this match to feel normal, not desperate. Once a side starts playing the memory of the last defeat instead of the ball in front of it, the rematch can drift away very quickly.
